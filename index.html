On the way to Autonomous Delivery

Our vision is an end to end autonomous vehicle designed specifically to handle item deliveries in urban environments. Current food delivery solutions depend on human couriers to achieve product fulfillment. If self driving vehicles were employed instead, delivery fees could be dramatically reduced. Our project focuses on the perception systems that would power such a vehicle.

What is Caravel?

The core motivation for this project is the concept of a compact, low-cost self driving vehicle designed specifically to carry groceries and takeout orders instead of passengers. Because the vision for this project is quite demanding, the scope of our capstone has been reduced to only include select software components of an autonomous delivery vehicle. Specifically, the perception system is chosen as the primary subject of our capstone. We implement vision algorithms to accomplish lane following, as well as various other modules such as actuation and planning, and test them on a 2019 Toyota Corolla Hatchback.
